Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 24, 2025

Introduction

At DevTech, we take your privacy seriously. We understand that the information related to your device and usage is sensitive, and we're committed to protecting it. This privacy policy explains in simple terms how we handle your information when you use our RKN Player application.

App Permissions Explained

Here's why we need certain permissions:

Storage Access

  • What it does: Allows our app to access video files stored on your device.
  • Why we need it: This is the core of what our video player does. Without this permission, you couldn't browse or play your own videos.
  • How we use it: We only access the video files you specifically select for playback. We never scan your entire device or access other types of files.

Information Collection

We collect minimal information to make our app better and provide a smooth experience:

What We Collect

  • Device details: Technical information like your device model, operating system version, and app version.
  • How you use the app: Anonymous data like which features you use most and how the app performs on your device.
  • Crash reports: If the app crashes, we collect diagnostic information to help us fix the problem.

What We Don't Collect

  • We don't collect your name, email, phone number, or address unless you voluntarily provide it for customer support.
  • We don't access, collect, or store your video content.
  • We don't track your location.
  • We don't record your viewing habits or preferences in a way that identifies you personally.

How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect helps us:

  • Fix bugs and make the app run more smoothly
  • Develop new features based on how people actually use the app
  • Respond to customer support requests

Third-Party Services

Our app works with these services from other companies:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display ads within the app. Through this service:

  • AdMob may collect information about your device and your interaction with ads.
  • This information is used to provide ads more relevant to your interests.
  • We do not share any personal information with advertisers.

We adhere to Google AdMob's privacy policies and work to ensure your privacy is not violated.

Privacy Policy: Google AdMob Privacy Policy

Firebase Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our application. We collect anonymous data about:

  • Number of app opens
  • Most used features
  • General usage patterns

This information helps us make informed decisions to improve the app and develop new features that better meet your needs.

Privacy Policy: Firebase Analytics Privacy Policy

Firebase Crashlytics

We use Firebase Crashlytics to help us identify and improve the stability of our app:

  • Collects information about app crashes
  • Provides diagnostic data to help us understand and fix technical issues
  • All collected information is anonymous and used solely for improving app performance

Privacy Policy: Firebase Crashlytics Privacy Policy

Apple App Store

We use the Apple App Store for subscription management and app distribution:

  • Handles subscription purchases securely
  • Manages payment processing
  • We never see or store your payment details

Privacy Policy: Apple Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalized ads by adjusting your device settings.

In-App Subscriptions

Our app offers optional subscriptions that you can purchase:

  • What you get: Remove ads and unlock premium features
  • How payments work: Apple handles all payments, not us
  • Your payment info: We never see or store your payment details
  • Managing subscriptions: You can cancel anytime through your Apple ID settings

Need help with subscriptions? Visit Apple's guide on managing subscriptions.

Data Security

We take several steps to keep your information safe:

  • We use secure HTTPS connections for all internet communications
  • We don't store your media files on our servers
  • We regularly update our security practices

While we take strong measures to protect your information, no internet transmission is 100% secure. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, but we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights & Choices

You have control over your data and experience with our app:

  • App permissions: You can enable or disable permissions in your device settings
  • Analytics: You can opt out of analytics in your device settings
  • Advertising: You can adjust ad preferences in your device settings
  • Your data: You can request deletion of any personal data we might have by contacting us

If you're in the EU, California, or another region with specific privacy laws (like GDPR or CCPA), you have additional rights. Contact us for help with these rights.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not designed for or directed at children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we might have collected information from your child, please contact us immediately so we can remove it.

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. When we do, we'll:

  • Post the new policy in the app
  • Show you a notification about the change
  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top

For significant changes, we'll ask for your consent when required by law.

Contact Us

Have questions or concerns about your privacy? We're here to help:

  • Email: support@rknplayer.com
  • In the app: Use the "Send A Feedback" section

We take your concerns seriously and will respond as quickly as possible.
