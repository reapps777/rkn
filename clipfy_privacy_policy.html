Privacy Policy

Our Privacy Policy

Introduction:

At ClipFy Media Toolkit, we prioritize protecting your privacy while you use our app, which provides comprehensive media processing tools such as video, audio, and image editing. This policy explains how we handle user data, required permissions, and the measures taken to safeguard your information.

Permissions:

The ClipFy Media Toolkit app requests certain permissions to function properly and provide the best user experience, including:

Storage Access

This permission allows the app to access media files (videos, audios, images) on your device so you can select and process them within the app, such as compressing, converting, or trimming.

Notification & Foreground Service

This permission enables the app to process files in the background and show notifications displaying the progress of operations like compression or conversion, allowing you to monitor status without keeping the app open.

Information Collection:

Data Collected by the App

ClipFy Media Toolkit does not collect any personal information directly from users. We do not store or upload any images, videos, or audio files to external servers. All files are processed locally on your device to ensure privacy.

Data Collected by Third-Party Services

Some external services we use may automatically collect non-personal information such as device type, operating system, app usage duration, crash reports, and approximate location. This data is used to analyze performance, improve user experience, and provide services like ads or notifications. These services include:

  • AdMob – for ads.
  • • Firebase Analytics – for app usage analysis.
  • • Firebase Crashlytics – for crash detection.
  • • OneSignal – for notifications.
  • • Google Play Billing – for managing subscriptions and purchases.

Please refer to the privacy policies of each service (listed above) for more details on their data collection and use.

Data Security:

At ClipFy Media Toolkit, we are committed to protecting user data by implementing technical and organizational measures to safeguard your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

  • • All media files (video, audio, images) are processed locally on your device without uploading to external servers, reducing the risk of data leaks.
  • • We use encryption and protection techniques within the app to ensure safe processing of files.
  • • We do not collect or store sensitive personal data on our servers.
  • • Third-party services we use adhere to high security standards and strict privacy policies to ensure user data protection.

Despite these measures, no electronic system can guarantee 100% data security. Therefore, we advise you to take appropriate precautions when sharing or storing your personal data.

Your Rights and Choices:

We believe in user rights to control their data and privacy. Accordingly, we provide the following options:

  • Permission Control: You can review and modify or revoke app permissions anytime via your device settings.
  • Data Sharing Choices: While the app does not collect personal data directly, third-party services may collect some non-personal information. You can review their privacy policies to control your options.
  • Unsubscribe from Notifications: You can stop receiving app notifications via your device settings or within the app if the option is available.
  • Contact Us: For any inquiries, data deletion requests, or privacy complaints, you can contact us through the communication channels available in the app.

Children's Privacy:

The ClipFy Media Toolkit app places great importance on protecting children's privacy. The app is not specifically directed at children under 13 years old and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately so we can delete the data and protect your child's privacy.
